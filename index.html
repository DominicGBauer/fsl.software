FSL

What?

The Functional Source License (FSL) is a source-available license that converts to Apache 2.0 or MIT after one year. It is designed for cloud services companies that want to make their core products as Open Source as possible without jeopardizing their business. More open than open core and less restrictive than copyleft, FSL provides everything a developer needs to use and learn from your software.
