FSL

What?

The Functional Source License (FSL) is a source-available license that converts to Apache 2.0 or MIT after one year. It is designed for cloud services companies that want to make their core products as Open Source as possible without jeopardizing their business. FSL is more open than open core and copyleft, and provides everything other developers need to use and learn from your software.
